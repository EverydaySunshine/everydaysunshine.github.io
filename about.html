About Us

This is what I do.

We are a registered 501c3 non-profit organization dedicated to encouraging and entertaining at pediatric hospitals and centers/events for the developmentally disabled. Our goal is to uplift and inspire through the use of comedy, music, magic, theatre and musical therapy. We're producing an original comedy/variety show called "Woooo!" - think "Saturday Night Live for the 6 to 12 crowd". It will be distributed to all 235 pediatric hospitals in the United States and hundreds of general hospitals in the Southeast. The show will also be distributed to over 700 local chapters of The Arc, the largest national organization of and for people with intellectual and related developmental disabilities and their families. Most of these facilities have closed-circuit t.v. stations and/or DVD libraries for children to choose from. We've been working on this show for two years with professional artists, writers, musicians and filmmakers.
